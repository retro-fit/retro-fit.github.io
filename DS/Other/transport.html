BUS


There are local buses in the airport that goes to Chicalim and Vasco-da-Gama, but they are not very regular and often crowded. Probably the best option is to take a taxi to nearby Vasco de Gama and then bus to Panaji. From Panaji there are regular bus services operating to most towns and cities in the neighbour states: Mumbai, Pune, Bangalore,.. These services are operated by Kadamba Transport Corporation (State Owned) and other transport corporations. Other facts on long trips: - The buses stops for dinner and breakfast. - Ticket may vary from RS 250 to 650. - A journey to Mumbai can take up to 12-16 hours. - Try to book your tickets well in advance
